The processing of data pertaining to the health status of individuals performed only on the grounds of their explicit consent.

4. The personal data of the data subject is used by the Ministry of Health and the competent authorities designated to combat the spread of the infection for the purpose of analyzing the dynamics of the infection:
- Analysis of the number of the data subjects showing signs of symptoms and the moment of their manifestation;
- Location of the data subjects showing signs of symptoms, history of their movement and the distance to other people;
- Informing the data subjects, where such information is target to specific individuals and / or groups of individuals;
- Other analyzes and visualizations of the information collected;
- Administration and operation of the App.
