The Bulgarian Ministry of Health and the National Operational Headquarters are making an appeal to all citizens of the Republic of Bulgaria with a request for assistance with overcoming the situation, caused by COVID-19.

In the current situation, the Ministry of Health receives information mainly about the seriously ill and ones with severe symptoms, who have sought for medical help. In order to produce the most accurate statistical model, and to speed up the closure of current measures and return the population to a normal rhythm, information is needed from a population as large as possible, which includes citizens who are not experiencing severe symptoms or any symptoms at all.

The purpose of this application is to enable all residents of the Republic of Bulgaria to get involved and help by entering their data and keeping track of their health condition on a daily basis.

Through solidarity at the individual level, and by participating responsibly in the process, we will be able to get through this crisis together faster, with smaller losses for society.
